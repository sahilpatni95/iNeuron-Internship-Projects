Why Calculate?
- Apply a filter(S) to an expression. 
Ratios/ Percent of Totals

Syntax -
CALCULATE(<expression>,<filter1>,<filter2>,....)
Expression is an aggregate
Filters add to or Override Filter Context
E.g
ResellerSales := Calculate([TotalSales],
Store[StoreType]='Reseller")
AllProductSales := Calculate([TotalSales],All(Product))
